Four 5V relayes together in a single board to control 4 loads simultaneously. The relays Can be easily switched by microcontroller or arduino.


  • Rated coil Voltage:5V DC
  • Operating Frequency:50/60HZ
  • Nominal Current:10A AC, 10A at 28V DC 
  • Maximum Switching Voltage:250V AC, 28V DC.
  • Power Required by the board:Vcc=5V DC(For relay coils),5V DC(For energizing every relay individually)
