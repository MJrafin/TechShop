Kit for making a basic line following robot.

 

For in depth video tutorial on making the robot, click here.


This kit includes all elements needed for making a line follower. You can modify it further by adding more sensors and components as you wish.

The kit contains:


PIC Mini Development Board 1
L293D Dual DC Motor Controller Red 1
Digital IR Sensor Array2 (V1.3) 1
Rechargeable Battery Unit 1
Male to Male Jumper Wire-Single 5
Male to Female Jumper Wire-Single 20
Auto Charger 1
Rotary Wheel 1
Nuts M3x20 4
Bolts M3x30 4
Bolts M3x4 28
Star Screw Driver 1
Hex Spacer 10mm (Plastic) 14
Cable Ties 3x150 1
Wheel 65mm (Rubber Tire) 2
Gear Motor 2
Robot Chassis Tee01 Black 1

 

Important: PIC Programmer is not included in the kit. You have to buy that seperately.

For assembly and coding assistance, you can watch this video tutorial. We don't provide any written code with this product but you can write code by watching that video.
