Arduino kit from DF Robot

DFRobot presents the Arduino beginner kit for those who are interested in learning about Arduino. Starting from basic LED control to more advanced IR remote control, this kit will guide you through the world of microcontrollers and physical computing. 
A DFRduino UNO R3 (Compatible with Arduino Uno), the most stable and commonly used Arduino processor, together with DFRobot's best selling prototype shield are included in this kit.
The latest version of Beginner kit has upgraded all the details based on previous version. Besides premium quaility jumper wires, double resistors, more Leds, 9g servo,IR remote transmitter and receiver, 4 more useful components are added: relay, motor, fan and potentiometer. Now you can make more fanstastic projects!
