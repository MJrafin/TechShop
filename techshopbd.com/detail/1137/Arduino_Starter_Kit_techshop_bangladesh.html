Beginner in Arduino Project?

If you are finding a starter kit for Arduino to start your journey, then we strongly recommend you to use this Arduino Starter Kit. This package included Arduino Uno and all of the basic accessories to make your journey happy.


Any tutorial with this  - Arduino Starter Kit?

Well. We will send this product to you with a DVD, that contains all the Arduino Project with codes and circuit connection diagram for practicing some experiments with Arduino. If you don’t have any DVD ROM, don’t worry about that. We also send the files to you through email after purchasing this product.


What are the Arduino Projects come with?

You don’t have to purchase any other component to complete the given Arduino Projects. We had an eye on the beginner like you. Thus all of our experiment is joyful like:

  • Experiment-1: LED Blinking
  • Experiment-2: Button Interfacing
  • Experiment-3: Voltage Variation with LDR
  • Experiment-4: Music Generation (Twinkle twinkle little star)
  • Experiment-5: DC Fan Controlling
  • Experiment-6: Digital Thermometer (Suitable for Room Temperature Measurement)
