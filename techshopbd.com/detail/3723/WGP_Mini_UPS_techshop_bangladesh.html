What is WGP Mini UPS?

Simply, this is a mini UPS for Router, ONU, IP Camera, or Robotics and Electronics Project to keep running when the input power fall.  This small and powerful device is also capable to provide emergency power backup to your internet modems, VOIP Phone Systems, Digital Cameras, Alarm Systems, POS, and many other devices with uninterrupted operation.


What is the Multi-Output of the UPS?

Well. This device comes with three different dc output voltages of 5V, 9V, and 12V. The USB output port allows you with a powerful power bank experienced to charge your smartphone or other electronic gadgets.


What’s about the Power Backup?

For a typical WiFi-Router, the backup time is up to 9 hours. Which is more than enough, compared to the demands. Although backup time depends on the load current, we hope the in-built 8800mAh battery will be experienced with a satisfactory result for your devices.


Features:

  • Outputs 5V 1.5A, 9V 0.7A, and 12V 0.55A.
  • Supports a wide variety of devices.
  • Up to 3 devices can be powered up at the same time.
  • Can be powered up with any 9V or 12V power adapter.
  • Backup of about 9 hours for a typical Wifi router.
  • Battery level indicator and power on-off switch.
  • Can be used as a power bank for the phone.

 

Product Packaging:

  • 1 x WGP103 Mini UPS
  • 2 x Male to Male Barrel Connector Cable
  • 1 x USB to Male Barrel Connector Cable
  • 3 x Barrel Connector Adapter
  • 1 x User Manual

 

How to operate this device?

Power it up with a 9V/12V power adapter. Then connect your devices to the output with Male to male barrel connector cable (provided with the product). When the electricity goes off, the in-built battery will start proving power instantly and automatically. During this state or during using it as a power bank for the phone, a quick double press on the power button turns output power OFF and a single press turns output power ON. When electricity comes back, it'll automatically switch to power adapter input power for output power and in-built battery charging.
