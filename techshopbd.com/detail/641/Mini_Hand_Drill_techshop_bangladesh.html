This electric drill is suitable for both aluminum plate, thin copper, board, plastic board, chipboard, printed circuit board, epoxy board, and can be used for drilling , polishing, cutting  etc.


Feature:

  • ABS plastic body and light weight for a long time work.
  • Quick change chuck device without any tools.
  • Different bits and chuck can drill hole of different diameter.
  • Grinding wheel for grinding and polishing cutting, etc.
  • Equipped with special blowing tool box, small volume, light weight, easy to carry.

 

Package Includes:

  • One drill machine
  • 5 steel drill bit (with a diameter of 0.5mm;1.0mm; 1.5mm; 2.0mm; 3.0mm)
  • 5 Chuck (with a diameter of 0.5mm;1.0mm; 1.5mm; 2.0mm; 3.0mm)
  • 5 Grinding Bit (with a inside diameter of 2.0mm; 2.5mm; 2.5mm; 3.0mm; 3.0mm)
  • Adapter (output 12V, 1A DC)

  • Rated Torque:530g/cm
  • Max. Drilling Diameter:3.0 mm
  • Rated Input Power:1A DC-12V
  • Rated Voltage:AC 220v to DC12v
  • No-Load Speed:12000/rpm
  • Weight:0.8KG
